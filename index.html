Welcome To Mother Tongues Network

Preserving and revitalizing Indigenous languages across Canada

Mother Tongues Network (MTN)

We're dedicated to preserving and revitalizing Indigenous languages across Canada. Our mission is to provide Indigenous language learning resources to Indigenous peoples across Canada through a free, accessible, and interactive app platform for Indigenous peoples to learn, share, and celebrate their languages and cultures.

Bridging the language gap

With a lack of accessible language resources and limited networks for learning and sharing, many Indigenous peoples, especially youth, are disconnected from their traditional languages due to historical injustices and systemic barriers. MTN aims to bridge this gap by offering a comprehensive platform that houses language videos, lessons, and interactive exercises in various Indigenous languages and dialects.

Join us in envisioning a future

Where Indigenous languages thrive, reverberating through communities across Canada, empowered by our inclusive and interactive language learning app. We foresee a world where every Indigenous individual, armed with the knowledge of their ancestral language, contributes to the preservation and celebration of Indigenous cultures, fostering deeper connections with their heritage and community.
