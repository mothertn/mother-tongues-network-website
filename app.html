Indigenous Language
Learning App

Check out these vibrant screenshots of the app we're crafting! While the design may evolve, we wanted to give you a preview of what's to come. Stay tuned for an exciting journey ahead!

Awesome Videos

The app is going to be video-based, because the youth really love videos. We are going to have really cool videos that you can watch and learn from. Some of the videos will be from really cool influnecers and even cooler elders!

Learn Different Languages

You will be able to choose from a lot of different languages. We will keep adding languages as we go. We are going to really connect with the elders and language keepers to make sure that the app is really good.

Track Your Learning Progress

We are creating really great lesson plans that you can follow. You will be able to track your progress and see how you are doing. You can also see how much you are learning and how much you are improving.

Not into that neurotypical stuff? No worries! You can just watch videos and learn at your own pace.

Want to help us out?

You could be a big help in shaping our app's future! Share your thoughts and insights by taking our survey. Your feedback will guide us in making the Mother Tongues Network App even better.
